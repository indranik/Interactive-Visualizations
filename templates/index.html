Belly Button Bio Diversity

Whats in your Belly Button?

Introduction

A team of scientists from North Carolina State University and the Nature Research Center at the North Carolina Museum embarked on a study in 2011 to explore the microscopic diversity of the human belly buttons. The study analyzed about 600 samples across North America and published their findings in a 2012 paper titled – “A Jungle in There : Bacteria in Belly Buttons are Highly Diverse, But Predictable”

This dashboard provides an interactive way to explore the data from this study.
